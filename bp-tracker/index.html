NHS Blood Pressure Categories

Every reading is automatically categorised using NHS/NICE clinical guidelines

Normal
< 120 / 80 mmHg
Elevated
120-139 / 80-89 mmHg
Stage 2 Hypertension
140-179 / 90-119 mmHg
Hypertensive Crisis
≥ 180 / 120 mmHg
